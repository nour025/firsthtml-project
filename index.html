About Us


Welcome to Chopifex Coffee Shop, where students and coffee lovers find their perfect blend of focus and relaxation! Established with a passion for providing a haven for young minds, Chopifex is more than just a coffee shop — it’s a community hub where ideas flow as freely as our freshly brewed coffee. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where you can unwind, connect, and thrive. Located just a stone’s throw from campus, we understand the needs of students. Whether you're looking for a quiet corner to concentrate on assignments or a cozy spot to catch up with friends, Chopifex has you covered. We take pride in serving a diverse menu, from bold espressos to creamy lattes, along with delicious and affordable snacks to fuel your day. At Chopifex, we believe in quality and care. Our coffee is sourced sustainably and brewed with precision, ensuring every cup delivers the energy and comfort you need. We’ve designed our space with you in mind, offering free Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, and a calm, inspiring atmosphere. Come and experience the perfect mix of flavor, focus, and fun at Chopifex Coffee Shop — where your goals are brewed to perfection.
